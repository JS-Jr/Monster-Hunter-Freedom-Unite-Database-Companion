Does not apply to the Fire component of Shelling, Wyvern Fire, Crag S and Clust S damage
Rec Speed +20 Damage Rec Speed +2 Quadruples natural recovery speed
+10 Damage Rec Speed +1 Doubles natural recovery speed
-10 Damage Rec Speed -1 Halves natural recovery speed
-20 Damage Rec Speed -2 Quarters natural recovery speed
Protection +10 Divine Protection 25% chance of reducing damage taken by 30%
-10 Demonic Protection 25% chance of increasing damage taken by 30%
Guard Up +10 Guard Inc Allows certain normally unblockable attacks to be blocked, such as Gas and Beam attacks.
Evade +15 Evade +2 Increases the invincibility period of an evade from 6/30 of a second to 12/30 of a second.
+10 Evade +1 Increases the invincibility period of an evade from 6/30 of a second to 10/30 of a second.
Evade Dist +10 Evade Distance Up Increases the distance traveled when dodging by 50%
Potential +15 Adrenaline +2 Increases defense by 90 points and Atk by 30% if the Hunter's HP is below 40% of its maximum
+10 Adrenaline +1 Increases defense by 90 points if the Hunter's HP is below 40% of its maximum
-10 Worry Increases defense by 42 points reduces Atk by 30% if the Hunter's HP is below 40% of its maximum

+10 Gunnery Master Increases damage on Ballista, Cannon, Gunlance Shelling and Wyvern Fire by 20%. Increases the power of Crag S by 50%.
Capacity +10 Capacity Up Increases the ammo capacity of Bowguns and Gunlance by 1. Adds a fourth charge level to Bows.
ShortCharg +10 Focus Speeds up charge time for Greatsword, Hammer and Bow. Longsword accrues Spirit faster
-10 Distracted Slows down charge time for Greatsword, Hammer and Bow. Longsword accrues Spirit slower
Guts +10 Guts Survives with 1 HP if above 64 HP and receiving an attack which would otherwise kill. Does not work if Paralyzed.
Fury +10 Emboldened Composite Skill: Adrenaline +2, Guts

-20 Calamity Chance of adding an extra regular reward slot on quest rewards reduced from 22/32 to 8/32
