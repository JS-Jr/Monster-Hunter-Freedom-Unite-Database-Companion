Great Sword Cave:

By using two Elder Dragon Bones, it is possible to mine the giant sword.

You can mine the sword two or three times per session, and one of the items will always be a Dark Piece.

The other items have a 60% chance of being a Dark Piece and 40% chance of being a Dark Stone.
